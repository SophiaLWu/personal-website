Welcome to my website!

I'm Sophia Wu, a self-instructed full stack web developer and artist on the side.

I graduated Carnegie Mellon University in 2014 with a combined B.S and M.S. in chemistry. It took me almost a year working as a chemist in the real world to realize that it just wasn't for me.

Instead, what was for me was the creativity, logic, and problem-solving in programming. I soon took several programming and logic classes back at Carnegie Mellon University as a visiting student to gain valuable knowledge in algorithms and data structures.

I've spent the last year teaching myself the ins and outs of web development, mainly using this awesome online source called The Odin Project. Through my studies, I've been able to put together some fun little projects which you can check out in my projects section.

Currently, I'm looking for a full-time position where I can utilize my technical and artistic skills and challenge myself to build some incredible projects.

In my free time, I enjoy drawing and painting, immersing myself in role-playing games, playing the violin, shredding the slopes, and pretending to be a food critic.
